Map authored by Xi Wang

The data was collected in the 2010 census

This map shows the geometry information and the distribution of owned-free housing units accross Kentucky in 2010. It provides the proportion of the number of owned-free housisng units and the total number of owned housing units for each county in Kentucky. Readers can observe the difference of the number of owned-free housing between counties in a straight way. A clear and correct cartographic symbology plays a important role in the data representation. readers capture the information fully depends on the visulization, so they would be easily confused by incorrect or unclear cartographic symbology. It decides the effectiveness of communication between readers and the information of a map. A map alsways trys to deliver objective data or knowledge. A lot of thoughts and applications can come out from one map. For example, people may use this map to analyze the prospect of housing market; people may investigate the situation of mortgage in Kentucky, etc. The map only deliver the objective information without any bias.
